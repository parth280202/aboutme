Our concept

Our concept is designed to help reverse type-2 or manage your type-1 or type-2 diabetes through diet only. We don't recommend or suggest any kind of medications or drugs. You can easily manage your blood sugar levels by keeping the daily glycemic load count less than 25. Avoid consuming high glycemic load food products like grains(wheat, bajra, jowar, ragi, etc.), pulses and any item made from these food (like poha, oats, upma, besan chilla, etc), & fruits like banana, cheeku, dates etc. .
